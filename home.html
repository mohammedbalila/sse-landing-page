About

SSE stands for School Students Exhibition It's an initiative by school of math students aimed to perform various activities An annual exhibition, Hackathon competitions with their different fields, scientific conferences, workshops and lectures and finally scientific forums and program clubs and many more Our goal is to close the gab between university education and the job market and to prepare students to face it. We also aim to connect theory with practical applications and keep up with scientific advancements
